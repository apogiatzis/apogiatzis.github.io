About

Cyprus Computer Society (CCS) and CYberMouflons proudly present the Cyprus Cyber Security Challenge (CCSC) 2021! CCSC is a Jeopardy style Capture the Flag (CTF) competition open to all Cypriot citizens. The challenges - carefully crafted by CCSC mentors - span a variety of Cyber Security topics such as Cryptograhy, Pwn, Reverse Engineering, Web Exploitation, Forensics and Steganography.

Engage in more than two weeks full of hacking, test your skills and win awesome prizes but also a chance to secure a spot in the National Cyber Security Team of the European Cyber Security Challenge (eligibility criteria apply)! Pre-existing knowledge is not required, therefore even if you don't feel competitive, join and share the fun with us!

Instructions

The competition will run on CyberRanges platform. Registered participants will receive an email with instructions on how to proceed from there. In addition make sure that you join our discord server for real-time support, and chit-chat with CYberMouflons/CCSC mentors. Don't be shy.


Every problem has an assigned point value, which is usually representative of our perceived difficulty of the problem. Your total score is the sum of the points you obtain from every problem you solve. In case of ties, the time taken to solve the challenges will be used as the tie-breaker. There are no bonus points, so do not attempt to find hidden flags outside of the challenges. During the CTF, the scoreboard will display the ongoing scoreboard. After the competition ends, we will announce the winners, award the prizes and invite for interviews for joining the national European Cyber Security Challenge team (only applicable to eligible participants). Note that all participants are eligible for prizes regardless ECSC eligibility.


Never participated in a CTF before? Need a skills refresher? No problem! We got your back! It is highly recommended to try out last year's challenges at https://github.com/cybermouflons/CCSC-CTF-2020.


All communications during the competition will be conducted through our Discord server. These are, announcements, IT support, hints and Q and As. The invitation for joining the discord server will be sent out via email and will also be posted here. Further instructions will be sent out to registered users via email when the competition approaches. Note that registration will remain open for the duration of the competition.

Prizes

Apart from the opportunity to secure a spot in the national team of cyber security for eligible participants, there are also generous prizes for top contestants!

More information about the prizes will be announced soon! Stay tuned!

Rules

  • [+] Use common sense. If you feel like what you're doing is disrupting the competition in any way, STOP doing it. Don't wait for us to yell at you.
  • [+] Don't post flags or solutions to challenges in public places before the CTF has ended. This will ruin the fun for everyone else. Solutions / write-ups will be posted at the end and we welcome submissions from participants.
  • [+] Do not try and attack other participants.
  • [+] Do not attack the scoreboard infrastructure. Do not attack the https://ccsc.org.cy or the https://app.cyberranges.com/ domains.
  • [+] Do not carry out Denial-of-Service attacks.
  • [+] Do not carry outT brute-force attacks.
  • [+] Any kind of challenge chatter inside or outside this server is prohibited. Any challenge related questions can be directed to one of the mentors/challenge authors.
  • [+] Be nice. Don't use profanity. Don't spam. Don't harass. Do not discriminate against age, race, gender, religion, nationality, ethnicity, sexual orientation, choice of Linux distro or anything else.
  • [+] If you break any of these rules, you could be disqualified. Decisions made by competition organizers are final.
  • [+] Flag format is CCSC{...} unless stated otherwise.
